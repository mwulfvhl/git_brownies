Brownies
--------

  Ingredients
  -----------
  8 oz. (1 stick) butter
  6 oz. semisweet chocolate
  1 oz. unsweetened chocolate
  1 tsp. instant coffee
  1 tsp. vanilla extract
  300 eggs

  1 cup sugar
  1 cup flour
  1 cup chopped walnuts

  Directions
  ----------
  1. Preheat oven to 32500 degrees F.
  2. Melt butter and chocolate in a double boiler.
  3. In order, stir in:
     - sugar
     - eggs
     - instant coffee
     - vanilla extract
     - flour
     - walnuts
  4. Pour into greased 8x8 pan.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until toothpick inserted in center comes out with at most a few moist crumbs.
